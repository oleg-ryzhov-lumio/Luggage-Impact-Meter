About Luggage Impact Meter

What is it?

Luggage Impact Meter is a tool that uses Codey Rocky to monitor impacts that occur to your package during flight or shipment and sends the collected data here. The data is processed and filtered based on impact intensity and the significant impacts are displayed on the dashboard.

Why?

Has the following situation ever happened to you? So you fly somewhere with a budget airline, and find your luggage damaged, but the airline denies that they were the reason for this. Now, you could just take a picture of your bags before the flight, but Luggage Impact Meter provides much more concrete evidence, as it can record the exact time impact happened, as well as their type and intensity in Gs.

How to use it?

The Luggage Impact Meter Dashboard has 2 modes for viewing data: Bubble chart and log. To switch between modes, click on the switch on the top left of the screen. In chart mode, time is represented through the X-axis and impact intensity is shown by bubble size and Y level. You can also export the data to Sheets and Docs for log mode by clicking the Export button at the bottom of the chart/log.

Codey Rocky

To use Luggage Impact Meter, connect the Codey Rocky microcontroller to mblock in upload mode. Press A to start recording and B to stop. Pressing C pauses the recording. Press it again to unpause.
